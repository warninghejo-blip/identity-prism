Vault / Staking Terms

Reusable template for any Prism product where users can deposit, lock, or stake PRISM in a Vault-like mechanic.

Do not present Vault participation as guaranteed yield. Spell out lockups, withdrawal rules, reward mechanics, and failure cases in plain language.

1. Vault participation

Users may allocate PRISM into a Vault, staking pool, or similar mechanism under the rules of [App name]. Participation may be optional and may change over time.

2. Rewards and benefits

If the Vault provides rewards, bonuses, unlocks, emissions, or weighting, the exact logic must be described in product-specific documentation. Avoid vague statements like “guaranteed profit” or “fixed APY forever.”

3. Lockups, exits, and redemption

  • State whether funds are locked or freely withdrawable.
  • State whether cooldowns or exit penalties exist.
  • State whether rewards are claimable, auto-compounded, or only cosmetic.
  • State whether VPN subscription redemption can coexist with Vault allocation.

4. Risk factors

  • Protocol or logic bugs.
  • Reward changes or discontinuation.
  • Pricing changes in cross-product redemption.
  • Smart contract, backend, or accounting failures.
  • Regulatory or jurisdiction-specific limits.

5. Product-specific variables

Replace placeholders like [lock period], [reward formula], and [unstake delay] before publishing this page anywhere.
