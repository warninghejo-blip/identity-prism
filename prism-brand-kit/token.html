PRISM Utility Token / Balance Policy

Reusable public wording for a shared Prism utility unit that can be purchased, used across products, and optionally routed into a Vault or staking flow.

This template is intentionally flexible: it works for a closed-loop in-app utility unit today and can be tightened later if PRISM becomes a real on-chain transferable token.

1. Current role of PRISM

PRISM is the shared utility layer for the Prism ecosystem. Depending on the product, users may acquire PRISM and use it for cosmetic items, ships, frames, boosts, unlocks, premium actions, subscriptions, passes, or other product-specific utility.

2. Cross-product utility

  • In-game cosmetics and progression items.
  • Vault / staking participation where enabled.
  • Premium subscriptions or service unlocks, including future VPN access.
  • Special redemptions, access tiers, or ecosystem-specific perks.

3. What users should and should not assume

  • PRISM utility does not itself guarantee future token liquidity or public trading.
  • Unless separately stated, PRISM does not grant equity, governance, or profit rights.
  • Each Prism product can apply product-specific utility, pricing, and redemption rules.

4. Purchase and redemption language

Public copy should state whether users are purchasing a closed-loop digital utility balance, an entitlement-linked balance, or an on-chain asset. If PRISM is used to buy subscriptions, explain whether redemption burns, consumes, locks, or merely records a balance deduction.

5. Migration to a real token

If PRISM later becomes a publicly transferable on-chain token, publish a separate token launch notice, updated Terms, migration rules, and risk/compliance disclosures before changing public representations.
