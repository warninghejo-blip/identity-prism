Cookie Policy

Effective: May 1, 2026 Last updated: May 9, 2026

Summary — We use a tiny number of strictly-necessary cookies plus some localStorage entries to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. No advertising trackers. No third-party analytics.

01What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored by your browser. localStorage is similar — key-value data scoped to our domain. Both let the Service remember things between visits.

02Cookies we set

We use only first-party, strictly-necessary cookies. There are no advertising or cross-site tracking cookies anywhere in the Service.

04Third-party services

Some third-party endpoints (Solana RPC, Helius, Cloudflare CDN) may set their own cookies in transit. These are operational only — TLS session keys, anti-DDoS challenges, edge routing. None track you across sites.

05Managing cookies

You can disable cookies at the browser level — but the Service won't work without the strictly-necessary ones (you'll be logged out on every page reload, CSRF-protected actions will fail). To clear our localStorage, open DevTools → Application → Local Storage → identityprism.xyz → Clear.

06Do Not Track

We honor the DNT browser header by default — there's nothing to disable, because we don't track you in the first place.

07Changes & contact

If we add or change cookies, this page is updated and the version-bump is announced via in-app banner.
