Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-03

Identity Prism provides on-chain wallet insights and visual identity cards. We only process public blockchain data and public wallet addresses that you enter or connect to. We do not collect passwords, private keys, seed phrases, or any non-public wallet credentials.

Data We Process

We process public wallet addresses, transaction history, balances, and other publicly available on-chain metadata needed to generate scores, badges, and identity visuals. This data is publicly available on the Solana blockchain.

Wallet Permissions

When you connect a wallet, you authorize the app to read your public address and request standard public account data. You can disconnect at any time from your wallet provider.

How We Use Data

Data is used solely to generate your Identity Prism card, insights, and sharing features (including Solana Blink sharing). We do not sell personal data or share it with advertisers.

Data Retention

Wallet insights are generated on demand. We do not store private user data beyond what is required for app functionality and caching public results.

Third-Party Services

We may query blockchain infrastructure providers to retrieve public on-chain data. These providers process the same public data to fulfill requests and do not receive any private keys.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at support@identityprism.xyz.
