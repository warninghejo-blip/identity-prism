01Who we are

Identity Prism ("we", "us", "the Service") is an on-chain reputation and sybil-defense protocol for the Solana blockchain. We operate at identityprism.xyz and via our Android client.

This policy describes how the Service handles information when you connect a wallet, scan an address, play games, or interact with our smart contracts.

02What we collect

We are a non-custodial protocol. We never receive, see, or store your private keys, seed phrase, or signing material. The data we do process falls into four narrow buckets:

CategoryExamplesSource
Wallet identifierYour public Solana addressYou, by connecting
On-chain signalsTx count, age, balances, NFT holdings, DeFi positionsSolana RPC / Helius
Gameplay dataScores, durations, input cadence, achievement progressYour sessions in our games
DiagnosticsAnonymous error logs, page-load timings, browser typeYour browser

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, IP-derived precise location, government ID, biometrics, or any document of any kind. We do not run KYC. We never ask.

03How we use what we collect

  • To compute your composite score. Your public on-chain activity is fed through our scoring algorithm to produce a tier (Mercury → Binary Sun) and badges.
  • To detect sybil behavior. Cluster patterns, transfer graphs, and gameplay cadence are analyzed to flag coordinated wallet farms.
  • To verify humanity through play. Game scores prove a real person is at the keyboard. Reaction-time and input-cadence patterns are validated server-side before any chain commit.
  • To run leaderboards and economy features. Coin balances, staking positions, NFT mints — all powered by your wallet identifier.
  • To improve the Service. Aggregate, de-identified metrics tell us which features are useful.

We never use your data to advertise to you, profile you for third parties, or train external AI models.

04On-chain data

When you mint your Identity Prism card or commit a score on-chain, that information becomes permanently public on the Solana blockchain. This includes:

  • Your composite score and tier at mint time
  • Earned badges
  • Memo-program attestations of game results
  • NFT metadata (Metaplex Core)

Blockchains are immutable. Once published, on-chain records cannot be deleted by us, by you, or by anyone else. Mint with intent.

05Cookies & local storage

We use a minimal set of first-party cookies and localStorage entries strictly necessary to keep you logged in across sessions and remember UI preferences (theme, language, last-viewed tab). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party analytics trackers. See our full Cookie Policy for the itemized list.

07Data retention

Off-chain data — gameplay scores, leaderboard positions, cached scan results — is retained for as long as your wallet remains active in the Service plus 24 months. Anonymous diagnostic logs roll over after 30 days. On-chain data is retained for the lifetime of the Solana blockchain (i.e., forever).

08Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, UK DPA), you have the right to:

  • Access the off-chain data we hold associated with your wallet
  • Correct inaccurate off-chain records
  • Delete off-chain records (on-chain data cannot be deleted by anyone)
  • Export your data in machine-readable format (JSON)
  • Withdraw consent by disconnecting your wallet

Email support@identityprism.xyz from a wallet-signed message and we'll respond within 30 days.

09Children

The Service is not directed at and not intended for users under 13 (or under 16 in EEA jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a minor has connected a wallet, contact us and we'll purge the off-chain record.

10International transfers

Our infrastructure is globally distributed via Cloudflare's edge network. Off-chain data may be processed in any country where Cloudflare or our service providers maintain data centers. By using the Service, you consent to this processing.

11Security

We use industry-standard security: TLS 1.3 for all transport, encrypted database storage, principle-of-least-privilege access controls, and routine third-party audits. However, no system is impenetrable. If a breach affects your data, we'll notify affected wallets within 72 hours via on-app banner and Twitter announcement.
