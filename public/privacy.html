Privacy Policy

Identity Prism Privacy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

What we process

Identity Prism reads public Solana wallet data, product activity, card mint state, sybil scan results, quest progress, game scores, and wallet cleanup metadata needed to run the product.

What we do not need

We do not require passports, government IDs, private keys, seed phrases, or payment card data to use the reputation product. Wallet signatures are used only to prove wallet control.

How data is used

Data is used to calculate identity scores, detect sybil patterns, show badges and ranks, prevent abuse, and keep the shared sybil graph useful for future scans.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Identity Prism team through the official project channels.
