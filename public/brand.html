04Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use the supplied phav.png at full resolution.
  • Keep clear space around the mark.
  • Prefer the logo on dark or transparent backgrounds.
  • Use the exact palette hex values.
  • Pair display Orbitron with body Space Grotesk.

Don't

  • Recolor, rotate, stretch, or add effects to the mark.
  • Place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds.
  • Recreate the wordmark in a different typeface.
  • Imply partnership or endorsement without permission.
  • Use the brand for sybil farming or misleading drops.
