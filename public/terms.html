Identity Prism Terms

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Use of the product

Identity Prism provides wallet reputation, sybil detection, games, and wallet cleanup tooling for Solana users. You are responsible for reviewing wallet actions before signing them.

Wallet actions

The app may ask you to sign messages or transactions. Always verify the wallet prompt. Identity Prism never needs your seed phrase or private key.

Scores and reports

Scores, sybil labels, and risk summaries are product signals, not financial advice or guaranteed legal identity. Community reports may be reviewed, updated, or rejected.

Availability

The service may change as the product, scoring model, and sybil graph improve. Features can be adjusted to protect users and reduce abuse.
