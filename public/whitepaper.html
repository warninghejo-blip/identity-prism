01Abstract

Identity Prism is an on-chain reputation and sybil-defense protocol for the Solana blockchain. It reads a wallet's public on-chain story, combines it with verified, skill-based gameplay, and produces a single composite score from 0 to 1000 — mapped to ten celestial tiers from Mercury to Binary Sun.

The protocol's central claim is simple: reputation should be earned, not bought. A score cannot be transferred, sold, or scripted into existence. It can only be proven — through years of genuine on-chain behavior and through real-time games that bots cannot consistently win at scale.

02The Sybil Problem

Every airdrop, governance vote, and "fair launch" in web3 is drained by sybil farms — single operators running thousands of wallets through every quest, claiming everything, and dumping the moment vesting unlocks. Real users lose. Protocols lose. Trust erodes.

  • Airdrops captured by farms. One operator, 10,000 wallets, scripted on-chain history.
  • Governance captured. Votes decided by whoever spins up the most addresses cheapest, not by the community.
  • KYC kills web3. The industry's only "solution" is uploading a passport — surveillance with extra steps.
  • Reputation has no portability. History on one protocol means nothing on the next; every project gets farmed all over again.

03The Prism Solution — wallet to identity

Identity Prism converts an anonymous address into a scored, badged, portable reputation profile without ever taking custody of keys, tokens, or personal data. The flow is Connect → Analyze → Play → Earn.

On connect, the Prism reads the wallet's full on-chain history — transactions, token holdings, NFT collections, DeFi positions, and behavioral patterns — and assigns a starting score and tier. From there, the wallet plays: skill-based games keep the score alive, grow it, and continuously re-verify that a real human is at the keyboard.

  • Non-custodial. The protocol never receives private keys, seed phrases, or signing material.
  • Zero KYC. No passport, no selfie — humanity is proven through behavior.
  • Portable. One score works across any Solana protocol that integrates Prism.
  • Earned, not bought. Score is non-transferable and cannot be faked.

05Anti-Sybil Game Filter

Anti-sybil defense is not a one-time scan; it is a continuous filter. This is the heart of the protocol.

When a cluster is flagged — shared funding sources, identical transaction timing, copy-paste behavior — affected wallets see their score drop and are pushed toward the games to recover it. Recovery requires real-time skill: reacting, aiming, and surviving. Bots cannot ace skill games at scale, so coordinated clusters collapse together and fall into the Black Hole, permanently excluded from drops.

The asymmetry is the point. A real human recovers reputation through play. A farm of 10,000 wallets cannot — there is no human behind them to pass the skill check.

06Ranger Ranks & XP

Ranger Rank is a separate metric from the composite tier. Where the composite score reflects who you are on-chain, Ranger Rank is an XP-based progression earned purely through play — unlocking gear, module slots, and ranked content.

07Badges

Twenty on-chain badges are auto-awarded by reading wallet history, game performance, and community contribution, organized into six categories:

  • On-Chain (5) — Early Bird, Veteran, Whale, NFT Collector, DeFi Architect.
  • Trust (3) — Verified Human, Clean Record, Trust Pillar.
  • Games (3) — Game Master, Achievement Hunter, High Scorer.
  • Identity Prism (3) — Seeker of Truth, Visionary, Binary Sun.
  • Social (2) — Arena Champion, Sybil Hunter.
  • Engagement (4) — Quest Master, Quest Hunter, Streak Lord, Explorer.

Badges minted on-chain become permanent, public attestations tied to the wallet.

08Architecture

Identity Prism is built natively on Solana and is non-custodial by design. Wallet reads are served through Solana RPC indexing; gameplay results are verified before any on-chain commit so that scores reflect genuine skill rather than spoofed inputs.

  • Identity cards are issued as NFTs (Metaplex Core), carrying score, tier, and earned badges at mint time.
  • Game attestations are committed on-chain as proof of humanity and as the primary source of Ranger XP.
  • Hub modules — Prism Scanner, Stellar Forge, Cosmic Vault, Black Hole, Wallet Arena, Leaderboards, and Prism League — compose around the same reputation primitive.

Blockchains are immutable. Once a card, score, or badge is published on-chain, it cannot be deleted by anyone. Mint with intent.

09Roadmap

  • Phase 1 — Core. Composite scoring, ten tiers, twenty badges, Prism Scanner, identity card mint.
  • Phase 2 — Prism League. Orbit Survival, Cosmic Defender, Gravity Runner, and Text Adventures live with on-chain attestation.
  • Phase 3 — Hub economy. Stellar Forge cosmetics, Cosmic Vault staking, Black Hole burn, Wallet Arena PvP, leaderboards.
  • Phase 4 — Composability. Public API and SDK so any Solana protocol can gate drops, votes, and access on a portable Prism score.
  • Phase 5 — Cross-app reputation. Shared social graph integration so reputation follows the user across the ecosystem.
